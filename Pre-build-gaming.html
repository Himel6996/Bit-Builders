Why Gaming PC?

Building a gaming PC is favored over buying pre-built systems primarily for cost savings, unmatched customization to exact needs and aesthetics, and superior upgradeability for future-proofing. It also offers the satisfaction of a personal learning experience and generally delivers better performance value for the money invested.
