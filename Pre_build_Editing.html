Why an Editing Build?

Building a video editing PC is favored over buying pre-built systems primarily for cost savings, unmatched customization to exact needs (especially regarding CPU cores, RAM, and fast storage), and superior upgradeability for future-proofing. It also offers the satisfaction of a personal learning experience and generally delivers better performance value for the money invested, particularly for demanding tasks like rendering and 4K+ workflows.
