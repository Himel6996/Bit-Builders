Why Streaming Builds?

A streaming PC build prioritizes powerful components, especially a high-core-count CPU (or leveraging GPU encoders like NVENC) and ample RAM (16-32GB), to simultaneously handle demanding gaming and high-quality live video encoding. Fast SSD storage, robust cooling, and a capable PSU are essential, with optional capture cards for multi-PC setups. The goal is a seamless, high-fidelity experience for both streamer and audience.
